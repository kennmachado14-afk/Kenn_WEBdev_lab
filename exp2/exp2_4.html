Color Format Guide

  • HEX: Hexadecimal notation (#RRGGBB)
  • RGB: Red, Green, Blue values (0-255)
  • Named: Predefined color keywords
  • HSL: Hue, Saturation, Lightness
  • RGBA: RGB with Alpha transparency
